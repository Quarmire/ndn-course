# Bug journal

The covenant's ledger: every real bug gets an entry, from Module 5 to the end
of the course (earlier entries welcome). Reviewed at every checkpoint. Honesty
over polish — a dead-end hypothesis recorded is worth more than a tidy story.

Template:

## BUG-NNN · YYYY-MM-DD · <where>
- **Symptom** — what you observed, verbatim (error text, wrong value, hang)
- **Hypotheses** — each one you held, in order, and what test killed it
- **Cause** — the actual mechanism, one or two sentences
- **Fix** — what changed (commit/diff pointer is enough)
- **Lesson** — what would have caught this earlier: a type? an assert? a log line? a test?
